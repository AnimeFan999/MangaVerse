Volume 3

Summary:

Team Z is pushed to its limits as they face Team V — the strongest squad in their block, led by the insanely talented Nagi and Reo. The match turns into an intense battle of skill, ego, and teamwork. Isagi steps up as a real leader, learning how to use his instincts and read the game to create goals. By the end, Team Z pulls off an underdog victory, proving that their growing individuality and teamwork can coexist — at least for now.
