Volume 1

Summary:

After Japan loses badly at the World Cup, a mysterious coach named Jinpachi Ego launches the “Blue Lock” project. High school player Yoichi Isagi joins 299 other young forwards, all fighting to prove they’re the ultimate goal-scorer. Inside the facility, teamwork means little — it’s survival of the fittest. Isagi starts to realize that to stand out, he’ll need to embrace his hunger to win, even if it means crushing others’ dreams.
