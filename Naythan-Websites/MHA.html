My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia is a Japanese anime and manga set in a world where most people have superpowers called "Quirks" It follows **Izuku Midoriya**, a boy born without a Quirk who still dreams of becoming a hero. After inheriting the powerful ability "One For All" from the legendary hero "All Might", he enters "U.A. High School" to train alongside other aspiring heroes while facing dangerous villains and intense challenges.
