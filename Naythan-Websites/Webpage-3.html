Volume 2

Summary:

After surviving the first elimination round, Isagi and his teammates at Team Z start facing tougher matches inside Blue Lock. They realize that talent alone isn’t enough — they need strategy, ego, and teamwork (even in a place that discourages it). Isagi begins figuring out his unique ability: his sharp awareness and ability to “read the field.” As Team Z fights to stay in the competition, rivalries heat up — especially with the powerhouse Team V and the prodigy Nagi Seishiro.
