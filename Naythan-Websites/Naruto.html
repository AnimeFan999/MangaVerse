Naruto

"Naruto" is a Japanese anime and manga that follows "Naruto Uzumaki", an energetic young ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest leader of his village, the "Hokage". Born with the powerful "Nine-Tails fox spirit" sealed inside him, Naruto faces fear and rejection from others but grows stronger through perseverance, friendship, and training. The story follows his journey to gain recognition, protect his village, and uncover deeper threats in the ninja world.
